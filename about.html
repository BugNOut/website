Our Story

Protecting Central Pennsylvania Since 2009

Bug N' Out LLC was founded with a simple mission: provide effective, affordable pest control with honest, transparent service. What started as a small operation has grown into a trusted name in pest management across Central Pennsylvania.

We believe in doing the job right the first time, using the most effective and environmentally responsible methods available. Our team undergoes continuous training to stay current with the latest pest control technologies and regulations.

Today, we serve hundreds of residential and commercial clients across the region, from single-family homes to large institutional facilities.
